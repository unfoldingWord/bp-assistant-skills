# translate-ordinal

## Purpose
Identify when ordinal numbers (first, second, third) may need translation notes because some languages lack ordinal forms.

## Definition
Ordinal numbers indicate position in a sequence or list. They are used for:
- Days, months, years in a sequence
- Items in ordered lists
- Position or rank
- Resurrection, death, or other events in sequence

## When to Write Notes

Write notes when ordinal numbers appear and translators might need to:
- Convert ordinals to cardinal numbers
- Use equivalent expressions like "day one," "month two"
- Reword to avoid ordinals entirely

### Common Contexts
- **Days**: "the seventh day," "the first day"
- **Months**: "the second month," "the fourteenth day of the month"
- **Years**: "in the six hundredth year"
- **Sequences**: "the first seal," "the second angel"
- **Ranking**: "first apostles, second prophets, third teachers"
- **Items in rows**: "the first row," "the fourth row"

## NOT translate-ordinal

| Use This Instead | When |
|------------------|------|
| translate-numbers | Cardinal numbers (one, two, 100) |
| translate-fraction | Fractions derived from ordinals (a third, a fifth) |

## Recognition Patterns

1. **Ordinal words**: first, second, third, fourth, fifth, sixth, seventh, eighth, ninth, tenth, etc.
2. **Higher ordinals**: eleventh, twelfth, fourteenth, twenty-first, twenty-fourth
3. **Context**: position in a list, day/month/year counting, sequence of events

## Categories

### Days and Time
- "on the first day" / "on the seventh day"
- "on the sixth day" / "on the eighth day"
- "the third hour" / "the ninth hour"
- AT: [on day 1], [day seven], [hour three]

### Months and Dates
- "the second month"
- "the fourteenth day of the first month"
- "the twenty-first day of the month"
- AT: [month 2], [day 14 of month 1]

### Years
- "in the six hundredth year of Noah's life"
- AT: [when Noah was 600 years old]

### Sequences (Revelation, lists)
- "the first angel," "the second seal," "the seventh trumpet"
- "first apostles, second prophets, third teachers"
- AT: [angel number one], [seal number two]

### Position in Order
- "the first lot," "the second to Jedaiah"
- "the first row," "the fourth row"
- AT: [lot one], [row number four]

### Resurrections and Deaths
- "the first resurrection," "the second death"
- AT: [resurrection number one], [death number two]

## Examples from Published Notes

**Genesis 1:8**: "the second day" - AT: [day two]
**Exodus 12:16**: "the first day...the seventh day" - AT: [day one...day seven]
**Leviticus 9:1**: "the eighth day" - first day after seven-day ceremony
**Revelation 8:7**: "the first angel" - AT: [angel number one]
**Matthew 20:3**: "the third hour" - AT: [hour three]
**1 Corinthians 15:4**: "on the third day" - counting from burial day
