# translate-fraction

## Purpose
Identify when fractions in biblical text may need translation notes because some languages lack fraction terminology.

## Definition
A fraction represents part of a whole. When an item is divided into equal parts, a fraction refers to one or more of those parts. Common biblical fractions: half, third, fourth, fifth, tenth.

## When to Write Notes

Write notes when fractions appear and translators might need to:
- Explain the fraction concept (one part out of X equal parts)
- Provide alternative expressions
- Handle compound fractions (three-tenths, two-thirds)

### Common Contexts
- **Measurements**: fractions of ephah, hin, shekel
- **Portions**: half of the people, a third of the sea
- **Tithes**: a tenth (tithe) of produce
- **Divisions**: half-tribe, a fourth of a hin

## NOT translate-fraction

| Use This Instead | When |
|------------------|------|
| translate-ordinal | Ordinal position (first, second, seventh) |
| translate-numbers | Cardinal numbers (one, two, seven) |
| translate-bvolume | Volume measurement context (ephah, hin) |
| translate-bweight | Weight measurement context (shekel) |

Note: Fractions often appear WITH measurement terms. Write translate-fraction notes for the fraction itself; the measurement unit may also need a separate note.

## Recognition Patterns

1. **Half/half of**: half a hin, the half-tribe, half of them
2. **Third/a third**: a third of the sea, a third of mankind
3. **Fourth/a fourth**: a fourth of a hin, one fourth of the earth
4. **Fifth/a fifth**: a fifth of it, its fifth (restitution)
5. **Tenth/a tenth**: a tenth of an ephah, tithe
6. **Compound**: two-tenths, three-tenths, two-thirds

## Categories

### Simple Fractions
- **Half** (1/2): most common, used for tribes, groups, quantities
- **Third** (1/3): common in Revelation for partial destruction
- **Fourth** (1/4): measurements, portions
- **Fifth** (1/5): restitution additions in Leviticus
- **Tenth** (1/10): tithes, grain offerings

### Compound Fractions
- **Two-thirds** (2/3): 2 Samuel 13:21 (sharpening cost)
- **Three-tenths** (3/10): grain offerings (Numbers 15:9)
- **Two-tenths** (2/10): bread offerings (Leviticus 23:13)

### Special Uses
- **Half-tribe**: tribe of Manasseh divided into two groups
- **Tithe**: specifically a tenth given to God/priests
- **Restitution fifth**: penalty addition (Leviticus 5:16, 6:5)

## Translation Strategies from TA

1. Tell the number of parts divided into, then parts referred to:
   - "a third of the sea" -> "It was like they divided the ocean into three parts, and one part became blood"

2. For measurements, use metric equivalents (from UST):
   - "two-thirds of a shekel" -> "eight grams of silver"
   - "three-tenths of an ephah" -> "six and one-half liters"

## Examples from Published Notes

**Leviticus 5:11**: "a tenth of an ephah" - one part out of ten
**Leviticus 23:13**: "two-tenths of an ephah" - two parts out of ten
**Exodus 25:10**: "and a half...and a half" - repeated halves in dimensions
**Revelation 8:8**: "a third of the sea" - one part out of three
**Nehemiah 10:32**: "a third of a shekel" - one part out of three
**1 Kings 6:31**: "a fifth" - one part out of five
